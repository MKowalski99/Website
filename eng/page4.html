Position: Store Clerk

Liber Pluteum welcomes applicants for an open part-time store clerk position. Scheduled generally alongside another clerk or backroom employee during regular working hours, this position includes varied duties. The bulk of these are comprised of Front Desk Attendant duties, such as handling purchases and general customer service, as well as maintaining generally cataloguing and databases on down time. In addition to these, during downtime or when more than one front desk employee is present additional duties may include organizing materials, shelving new materials, potentially assisting backroom workers, as well as other duties as assigned. The desired applicant will have been enrolled in or have finished High School(or equivalent), past work experience in customer service or front end sales (preferably within a library or bookstore), as well as a strong set of applicable skills.

Responsibilities:
>Front Desk duties including handling purchases
>Shelving materials and upkeeping organization
>Rudimentary maintenance of out materials database
>Assisting with opening and closing procedures
>Assisting patrons with any questions
>Answering phone and answering any questions
>Locate materials for patrons and fellow staff

Qualifications:
>Finished or enrolled in Highschool
>Ability to adapt to changing workplace/technology
>Inclination to public service
>Strong organizational skills
>Ability to function as part of a team

Qualifications:
>1 year experience in Library/Booksales (preferred)

Job Type: Part part-time

Pay: $15/hr.

Hours: 12-16/week
