Position: Store Clerk

Liber Pluteum welcomes applicants for an open part-time store clerk position. Scheduled generally alongside another clerk or backroom employee, this position includes varied duties, such as handeling purchases, shelving/organizing materials, assisting backroom workers, rudimentary database maintainance, as well as other duties as assigned.

Responsibilities:
>Front Desk duties including handelling purchases
>Shelving materials and upkeeping organization
>Rudimentary maintainance of out materials database
>Assisting with opening and closing procedures
>Assisting patrons with any questions
>Answering phone and answering any questions
>Locate materials for patrons and fellow staff

Qualifications:
>Finished or enrolled in Highschool
>Ability to adapt to changing workplace/technology
>Inclination to public service
>Strong organizational skills
>Ability to function as part of a team

Qualifications:
>1 year experience in Library/Booksales (preferred)

Job Type: Part part-time

Pay: $15/hr.

Hours: 12-16/week
